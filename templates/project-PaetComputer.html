LPDAAC External Website

The Land Processes Active Archive Center (LPDAAC) is responsible for handling of land data products for approximately 200,000 users monthly. Raw data is collected from satellite sensors, such as Advanced Spaceborne Thermal Emission and Reflection Radiometer (ASTER) aboard NASA's Terra satellite, and processes it for the public, and archives the products for future use.
