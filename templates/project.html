LP DAAC External Website

The Land Processes Active Archive Center (LP DAAC) is responsible for handling of land data products for approximately 200,000 users monthly. Raw data is collected from satellite sensors, such as Advanced Spaceborne Thermal Emission and Reflection Radiometer (ASTER) aboard NASA's Terra satellite, ingests it for the public, and archives the products for future use.

    • AESICS

    The ASTER Emergency Scheduling Interface and Control System (AESICS) is a web application that is used by LP DAAC Scientists. AESICS integrates real-time satellite monitoring of disasters to the acquisition of high-resolution remote sensing data. This app is powered by Python Flask and uses a caches requests from its database.

    • Listings App

    The Listings App is a dynamic website builder tailored specifically for individuals engaged in animal breeding, catering to both farmers and pet owners alike. Built with Django framework and powered by Wagtail CMS, the Listings App offers a platform to showcase and promote various breeding services. It harnesses the capabilities of OpenAI, allowing for the creation of efficient, informative content.
